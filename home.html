TURN IT ON FROM ANYWHERE

Turn Almost anything ON or OFF (like your heating) from ANYWHERE IN THE WORLD without WiFi and without Internet

We supply a Module capable of controlling a remote RF socket from anywhere that has a cell phone/mobile phone signal.  It can also be used where there is no cell phone signal and there is only a landline. (See use with a landline.)

You don't need complex apps or anything complicated. You just need to be able to make a phone call which is unanswered and FREE.

Suitable for holiday homes or other remote areas which don't have internet or WiFi but can be used anywhere where there is a cell phone signal or indeed a landline.

It also has the advantage of being secure because you do not need Wifi or Internet.

It can of course be used where WiFi and internet are available if you want to avoid the hassle of using these constantly changing technologies and value your privacy.

It operates by making a call to an old mobile phone whose screen is taped to a light dependent resistor(LDR) attached to our Module. When the mobile phone backlight is illuminated, as a result of the call, for a certain amount of time the Module transmits an RF signal to the socket turning it ON or OFF. The call is not answered so NO CALL CHARGES apply.

The Module can be controlled in 10 different Modes described in the Modes of Operation menu button on the left. These modes are selected by placing the supplied jumpers on the circuit board.
